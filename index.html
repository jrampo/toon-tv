Frequent questions

  • What is included in Disney+?

    Disney+ offers:

    • Premium experience without commercial breaks.
    • The best stories from Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars and National Geographic, all in one place.
    • Groupwatch: watch together with friends or family, even separated.
    • Unlimited downloads up to 10 compatible devices.
    • More than 300 titles in 4K UHD and HDR.
    • The possibility of watching on up to 4 compatible devices simultaneously at no additional cost.
    • IMAX Enchanted: available for select Marvel titles at devices compatible with Disney+.
  • Already have Disney+ or Star+ and want Combo+?

    When subscribing to Combo+:

    • The value of your existing subscription will be deducted from the price of Combo+.
    • If you subscribe to the monthly plan, the amount you pay monthly will be deducted from the Combo+ price.
    • If you have an annual Disney+ subscription billed directly via Disney+, it is not possible to switch to Combo+ at this time. Please contact Disney+ support to Know your options.
    • You will receive an email with detailed prices as soon as complete the payment.
    • Remember: you can cancel your subscription whenever you want.

    Currently, only the Combo+ monthly subscription is available.

  • What payment methods can I use?

    You can use: debit card, credit card, PayPal, Mercado Pago, and also partner companies such as Google Play Store, Apple App Store; Mercado Livre and local TV providers, telephony and internet.

  • How can I see content rated up to 16 and 18?

    To be able to access the complete catalog that includes content with indicative rating up to 16 and 18, all Disney+ users in Latin America must update control settings profile parent. You can restrict the content of each profile and also add a lock PIN. Users who prefer to maintain the current configuration will continue to enjoy Disney+ with an indicative rating of up to 14, and you can change it at any time in "Edit Profiles".

  • Where can I watch Disney+?

    The Disney+ app is available on mobile devices, browsers, video games, decoders and smart TVs. check out the full list of supported devices in the Support Center Help
